Welcome!

Admitt-ed is a platform for counselors and students to track their progress through the college application process. Students can research and compare colleges they're interested in, update their essay progress, and keep track of their scholarship information. Counselors can send out notifications and assign tasks to their students, among other things. We wish you luck!
